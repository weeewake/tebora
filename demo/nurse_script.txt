When the nurse logs in, she sees her patient list. Any changes in doctor assignment are immediately reflected in the list.

She initiates a page by clicking on the page button. She selects the type of issue, enters a brief description and clicks on the Send button.

On her Home screen she can see a list of recent pages she has opened and the status of each.

She sees that the doctor has responded to her earlier prescription request.

She clicks on the page to see the details. The doctor has filled the prescription she requested.

She returns to the home screen, and sets her status as busy for 15 minutes, thus ensuring that she is not interrupted while administering the medication.
